Viral Toot Simulator

Now you can pretend you have a toot going viral, without needing an expensive server or having to come up with anything clever!

Artist's Statement

A recent viral toot caused my laptop to boop incessantly. When I closed the Mastodon tab, I became anxious, no longer knowing whether people still liked my stupid post. Thinking about other ways to get the dopamine hit led to the viral toot simulator.
