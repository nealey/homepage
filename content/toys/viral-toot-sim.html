Viral Toot Simulator

Now you can pretend you have a toot going viral, without needing an expensive server or having to come up with anything clever!

Artist's Statement

I had a toot go viral, and suddenly my laptop was booping incessantly. When I closed the tab, I realized I was sort of anxious, no longer knowing whether people still liked my stupid post. That got me thinking I could easily simulate this dopamine hit, and thus was born the viral toot simulator.
